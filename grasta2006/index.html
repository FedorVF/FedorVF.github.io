TCS Special issue on Graph Searching

The 1st Workshop on GRAph Searching, Theory and Applications

Anogia, 9/10/2006 - 12/10/2006

The 1st Workshop on GRAph Searching, Theory and Applications will be held at the Anogia Academic Village in the island of Crete, a conference facility located in a unique natural setting, 40 min from Heraklion and Knossos and 50 min from Rethymno.

The target of the event is to bring together various researchers and research groups working with problems related to Graph Searching both from the applied and the theoretical point of view. A (non-complete) list of the topics that are expected to be discussed in the workshop is the following:

  • Fugitive/Helicopter Search Games
  • The questions of Monotonicity and Connectivity
  • Marshal Games
  • Conquest and Expansion Games
  • Graph parameters related to graph searching
  • New variants of graph searching
  • Min max theorems in graph searching
  • Cop-Robbers games
  • Pursuit-evasion games on graphs
  • Applications of Graph searching in computer science

Note that this workshop does not produce any proceedings and presentations here should not cause any problem for submitting the same material to a regular conference or journal.
